# New Employee Onboarding

**Policy ID:** VL-OPS-009  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** Day 1 to Day 30 roadmap for new engineers.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## Day 1: Access Setup

- [ ] Receive company MacBook Pro.
- [ ] Join Slack and Notion workspaces.
- [ ] Set up MFA for Vectix Foundry AWS environment.

## Day 2-5: Technical Immersion

- [ ] Clone the main repository (Rust/Solidity).
- [ ] Complete the "Security in Blockchain" internal workshop.
- [ ] First commit: Update your profile in the `team.json` file.
