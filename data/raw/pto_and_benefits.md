# VectixLogic Benefits and Time Off

**Policy ID:** VL-BEN-004  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** 20 days PTO and Global Health Plan details.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## 1. Vacation Policy

Full-time employees receive 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per calendar year. Up to 5 days can be carried over to the following year.

## 2. Health Insurance

VectixLogic covers 100% of premium costs for the "Global Health Plan" for the employee and 50% for up to two dependents.

## 3. Learning & Development

We provide an annual budget of $1,500 for certifications (AWS, Rust, or Blockchain Security) and Master's degree tuition support.

## 5. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **PTO** | Paid Time Off; vacation days per calendar year. |
| **Full-time** | Employment at standard full-time hours as defined in contract; eligibility for benefits. |
| **Carryover** | Unused PTO days (up to 5) that may be used in the following calendar year. |
| **Global Health Plan** | VectixLogic's medical, dental, vision, and mental health plan; see VL-BEN-017. |
| **Premium** | Cost of health insurance paid by company (100% employee, 50% up to two dependents). |
| **Dependents** | Spouse and/or children as defined in plan documents. |
| **Learning & Development (L&D)** | Training, certifications, and education support. |
| **Certifications** | AWS, Rust, Blockchain Security, or other approved credentials. |
| **Master's degree tuition support** | Company contribution toward graduate tuition per HR guidelines. |
| **Calendar year** | January 1 through December 31. |
| **Eligibility** | Meeting conditions (e.g., full-time, 30 days) for a benefit. |
| **Annual budget** | Maximum amount per employee per year for the stated benefit. |
| **Rust** | Programming language; see VL-TECH-016. |
| **Blockchain Security** | Domain of smart contract and key security; see VL-SEC-002. |
| **AWS** | Amazon Web Services; cloud provider for Vectix Foundry. |
