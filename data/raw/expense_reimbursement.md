# VectixLogic Expense and Travel Policy

**Policy ID:** VL-FIN-006  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** Travel stipends ($75/day) and software refunds.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## 1. Travel Expenses

Business travel must be pre-approved by the Department Head. For international travel between Nigeria and the USA, employees are entitled to Economy Plus seating and a $75 daily meal stipend.

## 2. Software Subscriptions

Individual subscriptions to AI tools (e.g., Cursor, Midjourney) are reimbursable up to $30/month. Team-wide licenses for AWS or GitHub Enterprise are managed centrally.

## 3. Reimbursement Timeline

Receipts must be submitted via the internal portal within 30 days of the expense. Payments are disbursed on the 15th of every month.

## 5. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Pre-approved** | Approved by Department Head before travel or expense is incurred. |
| **Department Head** | Manager responsible for the employee's cost center. |
| **Economy Plus** | Premium economy seating for international travel per this policy. |
| **$75 daily meal stipend** | Per diem for meals during business travel (Nigeria–USA). |
| **Software subscriptions** | Recurring fees for tools (e.g., Cursor, Midjourney); up to $30/month reimbursable. |
| **Team-wide licenses** | AWS, GitHub Enterprise, etc.; managed centrally, not individual reimbursement. |
| **Internal portal** | Company system for submitting expenses and receipts. |
| **30 days** | Deadline from expense date to submit receipt for reimbursement. |
| **Disbursed** | Payment sent to employee; on the 15th of each month. |
| **Receipt** | Documented proof of expense; required for reimbursement. |
| **Business travel** | Travel undertaken for company business; subject to pre-approval. |
| **International travel** | Travel between countries (e.g., Nigeria and USA). |
| **Reimbursable** | Eligible for repayment by the company per policy limits. |
| **AI tools** | Cursor, Midjourney, or similar; individual subscriptions up to $30/month. |
