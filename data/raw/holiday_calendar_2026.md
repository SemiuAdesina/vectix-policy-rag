# 2026 Corporate Holiday Calendar

**Policy ID:** VL-HR-007  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** Combined Nigerian and US public holidays.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

VectixLogic observes a "Hybrid Calendar" to support our teams in Lagos, Ibadan, and Wyoming:

| Holiday | Date | Region |
|---------|------|--------|
| New Year's Day | Jan 1, 2026 | Global |
| Martin Luther King Jr. Day | Jan 19, 2026 | USA |
| Eid al-Fitr | March 20, 2026* | Nigeria |
| Workers' Day | May 1, 2026 | Nigeria |
| Independence Day | July 4, 2026 | USA |
| Independence Day | Oct 1, 2026 | Nigeria |
| Christmas Day | Dec 25, 2026 | Global |

*Dates marked with an asterisk are subject to lunar sightings.*

## 3. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Hybrid Calendar** | Single calendar combining Nigerian and US holidays for VectixLogic teams. |
| **Global** | Holiday observed by all locations (e.g., New Year, Christmas). |
| **Region** | Geographic designation: USA, Nigeria, or Global. |
| **Lunar sightings** | Eid al-Fitr date depends on moon sighting; may vary by a day. |
| **Workers' Day** | May 1; public holiday in Nigeria. |
| **Independence Day (USA)** | July 4. |
| **Independence Day (Nigeria)** | October 1. |
| **Martin Luther King Jr. Day** | US federal holiday; third Monday in January. |
| **Eid al-Fitr** | Islamic holiday marking end of Ramadan. |
| **Lagos** | VectixLogic office location in Nigeria. |
| **Ibadan** | Location of UCH and pharmacy operations. |
| **Wyoming** | US office location. |
| **Public holiday** | Day off for that region; no expectation of work. |
| **Asterisk** | Symbol (*) indicating date subject to change (e.g., lunar). |
| **2026** | Calendar year this document applies to. |
