# Regulatory Compliance for Pharmacy Systems

**Policy ID:** VL-COMP-011  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** PCN (Nigeria) and FDA (USA) regulatory standards.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## 1. PCN Standards

Our software must adhere to the Pharmacists Council of Nigeria (PCN) standards for electronic record-keeping. Records must be stored for a minimum of 5 years.

## 2. FDA Integration

For US-based pharmacy clients, our expired drug inventory module must integrate with the Drug Supply Chain Security Act (DSCSA) tracking requirements.

## 3. Annual Audit

VectixLogic undergoes an independent security audit every October to ensure data integrity and system availability.

## 5. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **PCN** | Pharmacists Council of Nigeria; regulator for pharmacy practice and records. |
| **FDA** | US Food and Drug Administration; regulates drug supply chain (e.g., DSCSA). |
| **Electronic record-keeping** | Digital storage of pharmacy records per PCN standards. |
| **5 years** | Minimum retention period for records per PCN. |
| **DSCSA** | Drug Supply Chain Security Act; US traceability requirements; see VL-OPS-018. |
| **Expired drug inventory module** | VectixLogic software component for expiry and RFC; must support DSCSA. |
| **Annual audit** | Yearly independent review of security and compliance. |
| **October** | Month when VectixLogic security audit is conducted. |
| **Data integrity** | Accuracy and consistency of stored data. |
| **System availability** | Uptime and reliability of systems. |
| **Independent** | Audit performed by external party, not internal staff. |
| **US-based pharmacy clients** | Customers in the United States using our pharmacy software. |
| **Integration** | Technical connection (e.g., TI/TH/TS) with regulatory requirements. |
| **Tracking requirements** | DSCSA transaction and serialization requirements. |
| **Record retention** | Keeping records for the required period (e.g., 5 years). |
