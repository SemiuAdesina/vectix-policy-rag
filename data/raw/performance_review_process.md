# Performance Management Framework

**Policy ID:** VL-HR-008  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** Bi-annual cycles and EE/ME/NI grading scales.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## 1. Review Cycle

Formal performance reviews occur bi-annually in June and December.

## 2. Grading Scale

- **Exceeds Expectations (EE):** Consistent delivery above scope. Eligible for a 10-15% bonus.
- **Meets Expectations (ME):** Strong, reliable delivery. Eligible for a 5% bonus.
- **Needs Improvement (NI):** Performance Plan required.

## 3. Peer Feedback

Engineers must receive at least two peer reviews from team members focusing on code quality (Rust/Solidity) and collaborative design.

## 5. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Bi-annual** | Twice per year; June and December review cycles. |
| **Exceeds Expectations (EE)** | Rating for consistent delivery above scope; 10–15% bonus eligible. |
| **Meets Expectations (ME)** | Rating for strong, reliable delivery; 5% bonus eligible. |
| **Needs Improvement (NI)** | Rating requiring a Performance Plan. |
| **Performance Plan** | Structured improvement plan with goals and timeline. |
| **Peer review** | Feedback from at least two team members on code quality and collaboration. |
| **Code quality** | Clarity, safety, tests; Rust/Solidity per VL-TECH-016. |
| **Collaborative design** | Working with others on architecture and design. |
| **Bonus** | Variable pay tied to review rating per this policy. |
| **Scope** | Agreed deliverables for the review period. |
| **Formal review** | Documented conversation and rating in the review cycle. |
| **Rust/Solidity** | Primary tech stack for engineering; see VL-TECH-016. |
| **Mentor** | Senior role supporting juniors; part of Senior Engineer expectations. |
| **Delivery** | Completing committed work to quality and timeline. |
| **Review cycle** | Period (e.g., Jan–Jun, Jul–Dec) covered by the review. |
