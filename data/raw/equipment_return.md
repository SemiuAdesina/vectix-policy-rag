# Offboarding and Equipment Return

**Policy ID:** VL-HR-014  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** Offboarding and secure MacBook wipe procedures.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## 1. Return Timeline

Upon resignation or termination, all company-issued hardware must be returned to the Lagos or Wyoming office within 3 business days.

## 2. Data Erasure

Employees must not manually wipe the MacBook. IT will perform a secure remote wipe once the device is received.

## 3. Final Pay

Final salary disbursement is withheld until all equipment is verified and the "Return Checklist" is signed by IT.

## 5. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Offboarding** | Process when an employee leaves (resignation or termination). |
| **Company-issued hardware** | Laptop (e.g., MacBook Pro), peripherals, badges; owned by VectixLogic. |
| **3 business days** | Deadline to return equipment after last day. |
| **Lagos or Wyoming office** | Designated return locations for equipment. |
| **Manual wipe** | Employee erasing device themselves; prohibited. |
| **Secure remote wipe** | IT-initiated erasure of device data after receipt. |
| **Final pay** | Last salary and any accrued pay. |
| **Withheld** | Not paid until condition (equipment verified) is met. |
| **Return Checklist** | Form listing equipment and condition; signed by IT upon verification. |
| **IT** | Information Technology; performs wipe and signs checklist. |
| **Resignation** | Employee-initiated departure. |
| **Termination** | Company-initiated end of employment. |
| **MacBook** | Company-issued Apple laptop; primary device for developers. |
| **Verified** | IT confirms all items returned and checklist complete. |
| **Business days** | Weekdays excluding company holidays. |
