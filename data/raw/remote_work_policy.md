# VectixLogic Remote Work and Hybrid Policy

**Policy ID:** VL-HR-001  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** Hybrid rules for UCH Ibadan and Remote teams.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## 1. Eligibility

All software engineers and administrative staff are eligible for remote work. Pharmacy consultants working on physical inventory must be on-site at University College Hospital (UCH) at least two days per week.

## 2. Equipment Allowance

VectixLogic provides a one-time stipend of $500 for home office setup. Employees are expected to use company-issued MacBook Pros for all development work to ensure security compliance.

## 3. Core Hours

Employees must be available for synchronous communication between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM West Africa Time (WAT).

## 5. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Core hours** | Window (10:00 AM–3:00 PM WAT) when employees must be available for synchronous communication. |
| **Hybrid** | Work arrangement combining remote and on-site days; UCH pharmacy consultants minimum 2 days on-site. |
| **Remote work** | Performing job duties from a location other than the primary office, subject to equipment and core-hour rules. |
| **UCH** | University College Hospital, Ibadan; primary site for pharmacy consultants and inventory operations. |
| **WAT** | West Africa Time (UTC+1). |
| **Equipment allowance** | One-time $500 stipend for home office setup. |
| **Company-issued** | Hardware or software provided and owned by VectixLogic; must be returned per VL-HR-014. |
| **Security compliance** | Adherence to VL-SEC-002 (MFA, key management) and related policies. |
| **Synchronous communication** | Real-time interaction (e.g., Slack, video call) as opposed to async (email, tickets). |
| **Eligibility** | Criteria that determine who may participate in remote or hybrid work under this policy. |
| **Administrative staff** | Non-engineering roles (e.g., HR, finance, operations) eligible for remote work. |
| **Pharmacy consultant** | Role working on physical inventory or site-specific pharmacy operations; subject to on-site minimum. |
| **Development work** | Software engineering, scripting, or technical tasks requiring a development environment. |
| **Home office setup** | Workspace at employee's residence used for remote work; may include desk, monitor, connectivity. |
| **Main branch** | Primary branch in version control; see VL-AI-005 for merge requirements. |
