# Professional Code of Conduct

**Policy ID:** VL-HR-010  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** Anti-harassment and conflict of interest rules.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## 1. Mutual Respect

VectixLogic is committed to a harassment-free environment. This applies to all internal Slack channels, PR comments on GitHub, and physical pharmacy sites at UCH.

## 2. Conflict of Interest

Employees may not consult for competing pharmacy management systems or real estate platforms (Proptech) while employed at VectixLogic.

## 3. Disciplinary Action

Violations will result in a verbal warning (1st offense), written warning (2nd offense), or termination (3rd offense).

## 5. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Harassment-free** | Environment where harassment (e.g., discrimination, bullying) is not tolerated. |
| **Slack channels** | Internal chat channels; conduct applies to all. |
| **PR comments** | Pull request comments on GitHub; professional tone required. |
| **UCH** | University College Hospital, Ibadan; physical site where conduct applies. |
| **Conflict of interest** | Personal interest that could conflict with company duties (e.g., consulting for competitor). |
| **Pharmacy management systems** | Software that competes with VectixLogic pharmacy products. |
| **Proptech** | Real estate / property technology platforms (e.g., GIDA competitors). |
| **Consult** | Provide services (paid or unpaid) to another organization. |
| **Verbal warning** | First offense; documented conversation with manager/HR. |
| **Written warning** | Second offense; formal written record. |
| **Termination** | Third offense or severe single offense; employment ended. |
| **Mutual respect** | Treating others with dignity and professionalism. |
| **Offense** | Instance of policy violation. |
| **Employed** | During active employment at VectixLogic. |
| **Competing** | In direct competition with VectixLogic products or services. |
