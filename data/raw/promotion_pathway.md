# Career Growth and Promotions

**Policy ID:** VL-HR-015  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Focus:** Junior to Lead Engineer career ladders.  
**Version:** 2.0.1

## 1. Engineering Track

- **Junior Engineer:** Focus on task completion and learning.
- **Senior Engineer:** Design systems and mentor juniors. Requires 3+ years experience.
- **Lead Engineer:** Architecture and cross-team strategy.

## 2. Pharmacy Track

- **Staff Pharmacist:** Operational management of inventory.
- **Pharmacy Consultant:** Strategic oversight and system integration lead.

## 3. Promotion Window

Promotion nominations occur during the December review cycle.

## 5. Appendix: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Junior Engineer** | Level focused on task completion and learning. |
| **Senior Engineer** | Level with system design and mentoring; typically 3+ years experience. |
| **Lead Engineer** | Level with architecture and cross-team strategy. |
| **Engineering track** | Career path for software engineers. |
| **Pharmacy track** | Career path for pharmacy-focused roles. |
| **Staff Pharmacist** | Role with operational management of inventory. |
| **Pharmacy Consultant** | Role with strategic oversight and system integration lead. |
| **Promotion window** | Period (December review cycle) when promotions are nominated. |
| **3+ years** | Typical minimum experience for Senior Engineer. |
| **Task completion** | Delivering assigned work to quality and timeline. |
| **Mentor** | Guide and support junior team members. |
| **Cross-team strategy** | Alignment and planning across multiple teams. |
| **Operational management** | Day-to-day running of inventory and processes. |
| **System integration lead** | Owning integration of pharmacy systems with partners. |
